## Professional Philosophy

- **Transparency & Control**  
  I avoid reliance on opaque “AI magic.” Automated decisions should be explainable, auditable, and reviewable by design.

- **Zero-Trust AI Architecture**  
  I favor **zero-trust AI QA systems**, where correctness is enforced through strict architectural guardrails and deterministic validation—not just model outputs.

- **Pragmatic Innovation**  
  I bridge traditional QE rigor with modern AI-assisted techniques, ensuring ideas are evaluated realistically for production readiness.

- **Responsible AI Adoption**  
  I explicitly distinguish between **experimental AI capability** and **production-ready systems**, ensuring claims, implementations, and outcomes remain defensible and trustworthy.
