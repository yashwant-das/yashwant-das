### `PHILOSOPHY` &nbsp; How I think about quality

> **Zero-trust AI architecture** — Correctness enforced through deterministic guardrails, not probabilistic model outputs alone.

> **Explainability by design** — Auditable decision artifacts, confidence scoring, and human-in-the-loop validation at every step.

> **Production-grade thinking** — Explicit distinction between experimental AI capability and defensible, maintainable production systems.

> **Pragmatic innovation** — Bridging classical QE rigour with modern AI-assisted approaches, evaluated realistically for production readiness.
