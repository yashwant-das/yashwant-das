# Senior Specialist Quality Engineering | AI-Driven Test Systems

Greetings! I'm **Yashwant Das**, a Quality Engineering leader with over 12 years of experience architecting and implementing robust, scalable quality systems for Fortune 500 companies. My work spans **OTT / Media Streaming, E-commerce, and Fintech**, delivering resilient digital experiences across web, mobile, cloud, and device ecosystems.

In addition to classical automation, I specialize in **LLM-powered test systems and autonomous QA agents**—designing intent-aware, explainable, and self-healing test architectures that reduce long-term maintenance cost and flakiness. I focus on **production-grade AI integration**, not black-box automation, ensuring deterministic control, observability, and trust.

I have led the design of **agentic QA pipelines** that combine deterministic heuristics, text-based LLM reasoning, and vision-enabled models to diagnose failures, understand UI context, and autonomously repair tests—while producing auditable decision artifacts.
